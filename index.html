ABOUT INFINITUM

Infinitum is an annual inter school quizzing event conducted by the club that aims at testing the logical and reasoning skills of students while providing them with a memorable experience infused with fun and healthy competition.Taking its roots in 2011 as a small endeavour in Calicut,infinitum now stretches widely over Kerala, Andhra Pradesh, Telengana and even GCC.The event starts of with preliminary round followed by district finals and state finals awarding prizes of over a lakh rupees.To Sum up Infinitum is a 'One of its kind event' which is driven by an Unquenched desire to bring out the logicaland the out of the box thinking of the students.

F.A.Q

  • How many attempts are possible ?

    only one for both prelims and state level.

  • How many attempts are possible for treasure hunt?

    Maximum 3.

  • Does the student need to pay to register for infinitum?

    No, There is no registration Fees.

  • Can the student attempt the exam in mobile?

    Yes students can attempt via mobile/desktop.

  • Can we attempt the exam in any hour of selected day?

    Yes, Preliminary level would be accesible between jan 21 - jan 31.

  • What all would be tested in the quiz?

    Logical reasoning, mental ability, aptitude,memory and observation and all such related skills would be tested.

  • How will the ranks be if multiple students secure same score?

    Student who finishes in lesser time would be given priority.
